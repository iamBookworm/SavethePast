Home Game Facts

Instructions: Use the letters on your keyboard to control your character and change the world for the better. Incompletion of objects will result in a worse world for the future. Clean up your environment to make changes for the better.

Hints:

  • Spacebar to start
  • w: Water (if you choose Vanessa)
  • r: Running water (if you choose Dave)
  • l: Lights
  • t: Trash
  • c: Compost bin
