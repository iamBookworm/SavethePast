Introduction: Today’s world is polluted. There are many factors that contributed to the rise of pollution over the years. Although there are many simple ways to reduce pollution there has to be a significant amount of dedicated people. We decided to create this game to bring awareness to the actions you can make to save the world.
Background Story: It is the year 2118, the world is not in good condition. There has been much controversy over the impact of pollution in the world. There are flooded states, an abundance of gases, and many cut down forests. Your mission is to travel back in time to the year 2018 and make some changes.
